Our story

How the league got here

I'm Son, the original owner of this league alongside DevHub (bry8750). We've been working on football leagues for a long time now.

Back when it all started, the league wasn't called YSL. It was called OPFF — Official Professional Football Federation. Looking back, it didn't go very well. We restarted it so many times. We'd get active for a while, then everything would slowly die again. Owners changed, staff changed, and things never really stayed together.

Even through all of that, me and DevHub never fully gave up on it. We kept making new servers because we believed the league had the potential to become something much bigger. It did not work at the time, but we kept trying.

2023 - OPFF
The original leagueThe first version went through repeated restarts, ownership changes and periods of activity followed by long declines.
2024
The YSL rebrandOPFF became Youth Soccer League. It was more than a new name; it was a chance to stop repeating the same mistakes.
NOW
The returnAfter more than a year away, the league returned with better systems, clearer planning and full focus on the experience.

In 2024, we decided enough was enough and changed everything. We renamed the league to Youth Soccer League. It was meant to be a proper fresh start and a chance to build something with more care instead of rushing into another season.

After more than a year away, we've come back again. This time we're putting our full focus into YSL. We've spent a lot of time improving the experience, building better systems and planning things before launching them.

We're only bringing in staff who are willing to help push the league forward. That means people may come and go, but the goal is to find a team that genuinely understands what we're trying to build.

I'm not going to say the league will be perfect, because no league ever is. There will be problems and things we need to improve. What I can promise is that we'll keep working, listen to the community and try to make YSL something people actually enjoy playing.

Give us a chance. Give YSL a chance. Join now.
