OUR STORY

About Youth Soccer League

This page isn't about pretending everything has always been perfect. It hasn't. This is how YSL actually got here.

From Son (INOOBY6)

I'm Son, one of the original owners of this league alongside DevHub (bry8750). We've been working on football leagues for a long time now.

Back when it all started, the league wasn't called YSL. It was called OPFF (Official Professional Football Federation). Looking back, it honestly didn't go very well. We restarted it more times than I can count. We'd get active for a while, then everything would slowly die again. Owners changed, staff changed, and things just never really stayed together.

Even through all of that, me and DevHub never really gave up on it. We always thought there was something good there, even if we kept getting it wrong.

In 2024 we decided enough was enough and completely changed everything. We renamed the league to Youth Soccer League (YSL). It wasn't just a new name. It was meant to be a fresh start and a chance to build something properly instead of repeating the same mistakes.

After another long break we've come back again, but this time we're actually putting our full focus into YSL. We've spent a lot of time enhancing your experience, making better systems and planning things out instead of rushing them.

I'm not going to say this league will be perfect because no league ever is. There will always be problems and things we'll need to improve. What I can promise is that we're going to keep working on it, listening to the community and trying to make YSL something people actually enjoy playing.

Whether you've been here since OPFF or you've only just found YSL today, thank you for giving us a chance. Hopefully this is finally the version that lasts.

— Son (INOOBY6)
Founder, Youth Soccer League
